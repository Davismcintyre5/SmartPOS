USER DOCUMENTATION

Everything you need to know to use SmartPOS effectively.

1. Getting Started

1.1 Registration & Activation

  1. Visit smartpos.pxxl.click and click Get Started.
  2. Choose a plan: Free Trial, Monthly, Yearly, or Permanent.
  3. Fill in your business details: Business Name, Owner Name, Phone, Email, and Password.
  4. Agree to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.
  5. Click Start Free Trial (or Continue to Payment for paid plans).
  6. For free trials, your license key is displayed immediately. Copy and save it securely — you cannot recover it later.
  7. For paid plans, complete payment via M-Pesa (Send Money, Till Number, or Paybill), Stripe, or PayPal. An admin will approve your payment, and you will receive your license key via email.
Important: Keep your license key safe. It is required for device activation and cannot be retrieved if lost.

1.2 First Login

  1. Go to smartpos.pxxl.click/login.
  2. Enter your registered email and password.
  3. If your device is not yet activated, you will be redirected to the Activation page.
  4. Enter your license key to activate the device.
  5. After activation, log in again — you will be taken to the Dashboard.

1.3 Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard gives you a quick snapshot of your business:

  • Stats Cards: Today's Revenue, Transactions, Total Products, Total Customers.
  • Quick Actions: New Sale, Add Product, Add Customer, View Reports.
  • Setup Checklist: Track your progress in setting up the system.
  • Quick Scan: Use a barcode scanner to quickly jump to POS with a product.

2. POS (Point of Sale)

2.1 Processing a Sale

  1. Navigate to POS from the sidebar.
  2. Scan a barcode using a USB scanner, or click Scan Barcode to use the camera (on desktop/mobile).
  3. Alternatively, type a product name in the search bar and click the product card to add it to the cart.
  4. Adjust quantities using the +/- buttons in the cart.
  5. Optionally, enter a Customer Name and Loyalty Card Number (if loyalty program is enabled).
  6. Review the totals: Subtotal, any automatic discounts, VAT, and the final Total.
  7. Click Process Payment.

2.2 Barcode Scanning

  • USB Scanner: Plug and play — just scan a barcode, and the product is added to the cart instantly.
  • Camera Scanner: Click the camera icon to activate your device's camera. Hold the barcode in front of the camera. A beep sound confirms the scan.

2.3 Payment Methods

Click Process Payment and choose:

  • Cash: Enter the amount received from the customer. The change is calculated automatically.
  • M-Pesa: Record an M-Pesa payment (no STK push in POS — this is for manual recording).
  • Card: Record a card payment.

2.4 Hold & Resume Sales

  • Click Hold to save the current cart for later. The sale is stored in Held Sales.
  • To resume, go to Held Sales in the sidebar, find the sale, and click Resume. The cart is restored.

2.5 Discounts, VAT & Loyalty

  • Discounts: Configured in Settings → Tax & Discount. Global discounts apply to all products; specific discounts apply to selected products.
  • VAT: If enabled, the configured VAT rate is applied automatically.
  • Loyalty: Enter a loyalty card number to earn points. Points are calculated as Total ÷ Points Per Amount (configured in Settings).

2.6 Printing Receipts

After a successful sale, a receipt preview appears. Click Print to print the receipt using your configured printer. The receipt includes your business header, items, discounts, VAT, total, payment method, and a thank-you footer.

3. Product Management

3.1 Adding Products

  1. Go to Products and click Add Product.
  2. Fill in the product details:
    • Name (required)
    • Barcode (scan or type)
    • Selling Price (required)
    • Cost Price (optional)
    • Stock Quantity
    • Category — start typing to see existing categories or create a new one.
  3. Click Create Product.

3.2 Categories & Barcodes

  • Categories: View and manage categories in the Categories tab under Products. Rename categories by clicking the edit icon.
  • Barcodes: You can scan barcodes directly on the Add Product form using the camera button.

3.3 Restocking

  1. Go to the Restock tab under Products.
  2. Search for the product you want to restock.
  3. Enter the quantity to add and optionally a new selling price.
  4. Click Restock.

3.4 Stock Level Indicators

Stock levels are color-coded:

  • Green — Adequate stock
  • Yellow/Amber — Low stock (at or below threshold)
  • Red — Out of stock

4. Customer Management

4.1 Loyalty Program

The loyalty program rewards customers with points for every purchase. To enable it:

  1. Go to Settings → Tax & Discount.
  2. Toggle Enable Loyalty Points ON.
  3. Set the Points per Amount (e.g., 1 point per KSh 100 spent).
  4. Customize the Points Label (e.g., "Loyalty Points").

During a sale, enter the customer's Loyalty Card Number in the POS. Points are added automatically after payment.

4.2 Printing Loyalty Cards

  1. Go to Customers.
  2. Find the customer and click the Print icon.
  3. A printable loyalty card is generated with the business name, customer name, card number, join date, and email.

5. Sales & Reports

5.1 Viewing Sales History

Go to Sales to see all completed transactions. You can:

  • Search by receipt number or customer name.
  • View receipt details by clicking the eye icon.
  • Print a receipt from any past sale.

5.2 Processing Refunds

  1. In the Sales page, find the completed sale.
  2. Click the Refund icon.
  3. Confirm the refund. The stock is automatically restored.

5.3 Reports

The Reports page has five tabs:

  • Overview: Sales stats, payment method breakdown, inventory summary.
  • General: Total revenue, inventory value, payment breakdown, VAT, discounts.
  • Inventory: All products with stock levels and values.
  • Transactions: Filterable list of all sales.
  • Customers: Customer list with total spent, visits, and loyalty points.

Every report tab has a Print button for hard copies.

6. Settings

6.1 Profile & Business Settings

  • Profile: Update your name and change your password. Email is read-only for staff.
  • Business: Update your business name, owner name, contact email, phone, and address.

6.2 Currency Settings

Choose your business currency from 11 supported currencies (KES, USD, EUR, GBP, UGX, TZS, RWF, BIF, ZAR, NGN, GHS). All prices will display the chosen currency symbol. Note: This changes the symbol only; it does not convert existing prices.

6.3 Receipt Customization

Customize the header (business name and address) and footer (thank-you message) that appear on printed receipts.

6.4 Tax & Discount Configuration

  • VAT: Enable/disable and set the VAT rate (e.g., 16%).
  • Global Discount: A percentage discount applied to all products without a specific discount.
  • Specific Discounts: Create discounts for selected products only. Types include Fixed Amount, Percentage, Buy One Get One Free, and Buy X Get Y Free.

6.5 Loyalty Program Setup

Enable loyalty points, set the points-per-amount ratio, and customize the label. This controls the loyalty input in POS.

6.6 AI Settings & API Keys

  • AI Configuration: Choose to use the global HDM AI (provided by SmartPOS) or bring your own API key for DeepSeek, ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
  • API Keys: Generate and manage API keys for external integrations. Keys can be revoked at any time.

6.7 Staff Management

Add staff members with specific roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier) and assign granular permissions:

  • Manage Products
  • Process Sales
  • Manage Customers
  • View Reports
  • Manage Staff
  • Process Refunds

The owner (Super Admin) cannot be modified or deleted. Staff members only see sidebar items and settings tabs they have permission for.

7. Mobile App

The SmartPOS mobile app is available for Android devices. It offers all the features of the web application, optimized for phone screens.

Installation

  1. Download the APK from the provided link (or install via Google Play when available).
  2. Open the app and log in with your email and password.
  3. The app works offline — sales are saved locally and synced when internet is available.

Key Mobile Features

  • Barcode scanning using the phone's camera.
  • Bluetooth thermal printer support for receipts.
  • Offline POS with automatic sync.
  • Biometric login (fingerprint/Face ID).

8. Desktop App

The SmartPOS desktop application is built with Electron and provides an offline-first experience on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Installation

  1. Download the installer for your platform from smartpos.pxxl.click.
  2. Run the installer and follow the prompts.
  3. Launch SmartPOS from the desktop shortcut.

Key Desktop Features

  • Offline-First: All sales are saved to a local SQLite database and synced to the server when online.
  • Auto-Update: The app checks for updates and installs them automatically.
  • System Tray: Minimize to tray for quick access.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: F1=POS, F2=Products, F3=Sales, F5=Quick Scan, Esc=Close modal.
  • Direct Printing: Supports ESC/POS thermal printers via USB/Bluetooth.
